About IClass Building

Built for Quality.
Designed to Last.

IClass Building is a premier aluminum and glass solutions company based in Lagos, Nigeria. We specialize in transforming commercial and residential spaces with high-quality aluminum and glass installations that combine aesthetics with structural integrity.

Located at the Dopemu Aluminum Village — the hub of aluminum expertise in Lagos — our team brings deep craftsmanship and industry knowledge to every project, big or small.
